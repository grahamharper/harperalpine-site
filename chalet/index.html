The Chalet

Cabana Mira is a beautiful timber chalet built to accommodate large groups and families. It has plenty of rooms and all the facilities needed for a comfortable and enjoyable stay. The chalet offers:

  • 2 double rooms
  • 4 twin rooms
  • All rooms en suite
  • A large living room
  • A central wood burning stove
  • Dining area
  • Fully equipped kitchen
  • 2 lounge areas

The house is built on it's own picturesque plot of land surrounded by a spacious green lawn. Located just 4km from the ski slopes and 6km from the centre of Borsa everything you would need is nearby. Visit our gallery for more photos and details about the chalet.
